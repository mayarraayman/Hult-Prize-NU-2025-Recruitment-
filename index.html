About Hult Prize at Nile University

Shape the Future of Social Entrepreneurship

The Hult Prize is a global movement that challenges students to solve the world's most pressing issues through social entrepreneurship. Each year, the competition focuses on a specific theme aligned with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals.

As a board member, you'll have the opportunity to develop leadership skills, manage a prestigious campus competition, and connect with a global network of changemakers.

We're looking for passionate students who want to make a real impact on campus and beyond. If you're ready to take on a challenge and help empower the next generation of entrepreneurs, we want you on our team!
